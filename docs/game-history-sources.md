## Research portals

Use these when scouting or documenting other games (not shown on the landing page):

- [Ludii Games Library](https://ludii.games/library.php) — searchable catalog of traditional and reconstructed games (rules, regions, related titles). Start here when looking for candidates or ethnographic notes.

## Makopa

- [Wiktionary / Kaikki: Swahili makopa (hearts)](https://kaikki.org/dictionary/Swahili/meaning/m/ma/makopa.html)
- [Pagat: card games in Cameroon](https://www.pagat.com/national/cameroon.html)
- Local vernacular (Congo): players may call this kind of card play "poker congolais", and sometimes "kopo" in everyday speech. Product name stays Makopa on Bobeta.

## Kopo

- [World Draughts Federation: international draughts overview](https://www.fmjd.org/)
- [Ludii / draughts family documentation](https://ludii.games/)
- Local vernacular (Congo): "kopo" used as a familiar name for checkers / jeux de dames.
